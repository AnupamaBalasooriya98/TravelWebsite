Sabaragamuwa Dancing Tradition




Sabaragamuwa dance tradition was originated on Sabaragamuwa province which is located middle of the country above the Southern province. Rathnapura is the main city which is the main place for gems, especially blue sapphire. Actualy meaning of Rathnapura is the city full of gems. DAULA is the main percussion instrument that is used by the accompanist. Male and female both are performing the dance. The most flexible dainty and peaceful dance style in Sri Lanka.
